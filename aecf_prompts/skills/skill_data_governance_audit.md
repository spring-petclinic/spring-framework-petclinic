## Skill ID
`aecf_data_governance_audit`

## Description
Specialized audit to validate data governance in artifacts, pipelines and system flows: classification, lineage traceability, retention, minimization and operational controls.

## Objective
Ensure that all scope changes and artifacts meet data governance requirements with auditable evidence and concrete mitigation actions.

## Inputs

### Mandatory:
- **Scope**: module, service, folder or feature to evaluate
- **TOPIC** (optional): analysis identifier

### Optional:
- Current data classification taxonomy
- Retention and deletion policy
- Inventory of external sources/systems
- Previous audit evidence

## Steps

### Step 1: Scope & Data Inventory
Identify data sets, sources, destinations and processes that transform or store them.

### Step 2: Classification Validation
Assign or verify classification by data set and review classification rationale.

### Step 3: Lineage & Traceability Review
Evaluate end-to-end traceability (source → transform → sink), including responsible parties and control points.

### Step 4: Retention & Handling Assessment
Check retention, deletion, encryption, masking, and minimum necessary access policies.

### Step 5: Gap Analysis & Risk Rating
Classify gaps by severity (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH), impact, probability and evidence.

### Step 6: Action Plan & Output
Define prioritized remediation plan and generate the final document.
