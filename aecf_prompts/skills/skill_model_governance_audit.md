## Skill ID
`aecf_model_governance_audit`

## Description
Model governance audit to validate impact on behavior, inference paths, explainability and auditable evidence of AI-assisted decisions.

## Objective
Guarantee traceability and control over changes that affect the behavior of the model, ensuring reproducible and auditable decisions.

## Inputs

### Mandatory:
- **Scope**: components, prompts, pipelines or modules with AI behavior
- **TOPIC** (optional): analysis identifier

### Optional:
- Versioning of models/prompts
- Inference acceptance criteria
- Drift monitoring strategy
- Evidence from regression testing

## Steps

### Step 1: Impact Surface Identification
Identify changes that may alter predictions, ranking, classification, routing or response selection.

### Step 2: Inference Path Mapping
Document inference paths (inputs, preprocessing, decision, postprocessing) and points of possible non-determinism.

### Step 3: Determinism & Auditability Check
Validate reproducibility of outputs under the same inputs and existence of logs/artifacts for auditing.

### Step 4: Governance Controls Review
Check version control, change approval, validation evidence and explicit ownership.

### Step 5: Model Impact Declaration
Issue formal statement `Model Impact: YES/NO` with reasons, evidence and residual risks.

### Step 6: Recommendations & Output
Prioritize control actions and generate audit report.
