## Skill ID
`aecf_ai_risk_assessment`

## Description
Structured AI risk assessment across multiple dimensions with prioritization, mitigations, and traceability for executive decisions.

## Objective
Reduce exposure to AI failures through early identification, objective scoring, and actionable mitigation plan.

## Inputs

### Mandatory:
- **Scope**: feature, module or flow with AI components
- **TOPIC** (opcional): identificador del assessment

### Optional:
- External dependencies on AI or APIs
- Existing security controls
- Applicable regulatory requirements
- History of incidents or alerts

## Steps

### Step 1: Risk Surface Definition
Define assets, system limits and relevant failure scenarios.

### Step 2: Threat & Failure Identification
Catalog risks by category: operational, security, compliance, reliability.

### Step 3: Scoring & Classification
Evaluate probability and impact for each risk and classify them as LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH.

### Step 4: Mitigation Design
Define preventive/detective/corrective controls and implementation due dates.

### Step 5: Residual Risk Evaluation
Calculate residual risk after mitigation and acceptance/escalation criteria.

### Step 6: Risk Register Output
Generate prioritized risk register with owners and recommended status.
