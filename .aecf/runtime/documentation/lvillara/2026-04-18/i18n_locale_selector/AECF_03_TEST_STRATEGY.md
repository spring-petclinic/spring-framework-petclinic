## MockMvc Setup Notes

- **`webAppContextSetup`** includes all registered interceptors (LocaleChangeInterceptor) and resolvers (CookieLocaleResolver).
- View rendering (`InternalResourceViewResolver` → JSP forward) is recorded as `forwardedUrl()` in MockMvc without actual JSP execution — tests do not fail due to absent JSP engine.
- Cookie assertions use `MockMvcResultMatchers.cookie()`.
